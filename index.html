About Me

Robotics Researcher & Engineering Student

I'm a final-year Mechanical Engineering student at LAUTECH with a CGPA of 4.37/5.00 (Top 4%), specializing in robotics and autonomous systems. My research focuses on developing safe, intelligent robotic solutions that can interact seamlessly with humans and environments.

As the lead of the Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Innovator Network (RAIN-IN) and President of SPE LAUTECH Chapter, I'm committed to advancing robotics education and research in Nigeria and beyond.

My work spans from embedded AI and real-time systems to digital twins and simulation, always with an emphasis on safety, reliability, and practical applications in agriculture, manufacturing, and service robotics.
